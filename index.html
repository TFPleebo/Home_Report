2. Solar charging (optional)

Assumes ~65% real-world efficiency vs. the 400W rating (panel angle, clouds, charge-controller loss) — not the nameplate spec. This adds charge back in during the day rather than a one-time boost, so it matters most for outages longer than 24 hours.

Estimated runtime by station

Each station can only run on the battery capacity actually stationed there — a fridge in the kitchen can't draw on a battery upstairs. This is the realistic view; the combined totals above assume everything could share power, which it can't.
Assumes ~10% inverter/conversion loss per station. Real-world runtime varies with cycling, temperature, and inverter idle draw (your March outage test showed the fridge alone using less than the flat estimate).
